VAW is an independent organization, with chapters at international universities.

Our focus is to help ambitious students grant communities access to medical treatment, medication, nutritional security, and clean water worldwide.

More

VAW leads humanitarian initiatives focused on healthcare & health education.

Members travel worldwide to VAW clinics in underserved areas, taking on projects— while broadening their own cultural and career horizons.

More

The VAW@UCSC chapter also extends its efforts locally within Santa Cruz county.

The ultimate goal is to recognize local and global perspectives, allowing a more comprehensive look into the healthcare system as a whole.
