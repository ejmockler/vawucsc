Equipment and Textbook Drives
During the 2016-2017 school year, VAW-UCSC will hold an equipment and a textbook drive in which all proceedings will be brought and given to the community in our host country. 

We are also currently in contact with local bookstores and toy stores, collecting as many resources as we can get! 
* The dates listed below are still tentative, please check again by the end of January to have the correct confirmed information!
equipment drive
Please come join us at our equipment drive! Bring your old toys, sports gear, school supplies, drawing kits, markers, crayons, etc. Any donations are highly appreciated. 

We will also be selling baked goods at this event!

Proposed Date: Thursday, February 16 , 2017
​Proposed Time: 2:00PM-4:00PM
Proposed Location: Quarry Plaza

TEXTBOOK DRIVE
Please come join us at our textbook drive!  Bring any old textbooks, storybooks, dictionaries, writing guides, etc! Any donations are highly appreciated! 
​
VAW at UCSC will also be selling baked good at this event 

Proposed Date: Tuesday, March 14, 2017
Proposed Time: 12:00PM-4:00PM
Proposed Location: Quarry Plaza

TOOTHBRUSHES

March 9, 2017 
​Thank you Global Grins for their kind donation of 200 toothbrushes that VAW-UCSC will be bringing with them on their trip to Cusco, Peru this Summer!
SCRUBS
