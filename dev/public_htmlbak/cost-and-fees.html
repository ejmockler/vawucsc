Fee Coverage

Program fees cover, while in your host country: 
  • Housing
  • 3 meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner)
  • Clinic Transportation
  • Staff 
  • Transportation to and from the airport (only if you are with group) 

Medications and Supplies Fund

In addition to the program fee and costs, each member is required to raise at least $100 towards the medications and supplies fund in which this money will be used to purchase medical supplies for the communities in our host country. For the full list, please proceed here.
