Meet the 2017-2018 Board

Joey Wong
President

My name is Joey Wong and I am a biochemistry and molecular biology, linguistics double major. My hobbies are playing softball, fishing, going on hikes, and eating/snacking more than I should be eating!  I am passionate about global health and the medical field and learning from different cultures in and outside of my community.  Through Volunteers Around the World, I look forward in contributing to an initiative that advocates for those in vulnerable communities abroad, bringing attention to the people in these parts of the world that do not have the access to the educational and medical resources we have the privilege of getting. I look forward to using these experiences to continue expanding my interest in global health. 

Elizabeth Hu
Treasurer

Hi! I'm Lizzie Hu and I am currently a sophomore studying neuroscience at UCSC. Outside of VAW, I enjoy playing many sports including soccer, running, and swimming. I also like to participate in half-marathons with my family. :) I've always strived to help the community, from hosting blood drives and canned food drives to organizing charity fashion shows.  I was drawn to VAW because of all the opportunities it offers. I began cultivating an interest in the STEM field in high school, taking all the science courses that they offered from AP Chem/Biology to anatomy. VAW will lead me to further explore my interests through its incredible internship opportunities, which will not only give me experience but will also open my eyes to the real world and teach me valuable life skills.​

Michael Richie
Events and Trip Coordinator

Hi! My name is Michael Richie, and I'm one of the newer Board Members for the 2017-18 UCSC academic year. I'ma Sophomore currently majoring in Biology, but am always weighing my options between chemistry, neuroscience, and even economics. When I'm not in class or working at Porter Dining, you'll probably find me running track and field or on Pinterest. I look forward to this great year ahead of us! 

Brittany Davidson
Secretary

I am passionate about helping better the lives of others, especially those who need it most. I just want to make the world a better place and I think that starts with helping those most in need. I'm also passionate about science and the medical field in particular and I would love to get hands on experience. A combination of the two is like a dream come true for me.​

Vianne Kelly
Publicity Chair

My name is Vianne Kelly and I am a proposed human biology major at UC Santa Cruz. I am from a small town in southern California that values being there for one another and working together to help others achieve their goals. The city of Santa Cruz very much reminds me of my hometown because I noticed how every person looks out for one another out of the kindness of their heart, which is why I joined VAW in hope to also make a positive impact on my new home community and the locations around the world we shall travel to. When I am home from school I am back in my home town enjoying time with my family and working as an LA City lifeguard. Along with being the publicity chair of Volunteers Around the World, I am also on the UCSC Women's Club water polo team and volunteering with Bay View elementary school to tutoring children after school. ​

Eric Mockler
Social Medial Coordinator: Webdesign

Hey. Catch me behind a screen working this site, taking a photo somewhere, gutting a computer, or shredding these glorious Santa Cruz bike trails or a guitar. Sure, I might be a computer science major at UCSC hanging here with a bunch of bio majors-- but I see an awareness of healthcare & medicine as being invaluable for anyone to have. The emergent disparities between healthcare systems worldwide and at home is nothing to sleep easy over. VAW aims to close the gap, and sends us to communities in need with the tools we need to help. It’s a good thing to be a part of.

Adianez Ampil
Social Media Coordinator

Hi! I’m Adianez and I am Co-Social Media Coordinator for VAW. As a Human Biology major at UCSC, I seek to receive a higher education in order to pursue my passion for the medical field, and in turn, give back to communities around the world. Through VAW, I get to do exactly that. VAW allows me to volunteer my time to help local doctors provide medical care to those who may otherwise not have access to it. In my free time, I like to go on adventures and admire nature.

Jose Orellana 
Co-Fundraising Chair

Hi, I'm Jose Orellana, Co-fundraising Char of VAW at UCSC. I'm a Cognitive Science and Business Management Economics double major. I joined VAW because of its mission to improving the standard of living in communities around the world through medical outreach. I believe we all have a duty to help those around us and far away and VAW gives me the opportunity to do both. Besides loving to be a student at UCSC, I enjoy spending my free time between reading, Netflix, and going on hikes around Santa Cruz. I look forward to serving as a board member this year and hope we have a great year.

Irvin Reyes
Co-Fundraising Chair

Hi! My name is Irvin Reyes and I am a biology major here at UCSC. My hobbies include playing the clarinet, watching and performing in theater, and even traveling. I love seeing all that the world has to offer which is a big as to why I joined VAW. I can’t wait to see what this next year holds, because aside from traveling, I’m excited to learn and bring attention to different cultures and health disparities that most people aren’t aware of. Education, to even basic medical needs, are just a few of the services lacking from these underfunded communities. I hope to help with all this and more as I continue aspiring towards my dream of becoming a doctor.
