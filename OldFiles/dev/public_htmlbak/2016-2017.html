Meet our 2016-2017 Board Members​

President

Joey Wong

   My name is Joey Wong and I am a biochemistry and molecular biology major. My hobbies are playing softball, going on hikes, and eating/snacking more than I should be eating!  I am passionate about global health and the medical field and learning from different cultures in and outside of my community.  Through Volunteers Around the World, I look forward in contributing to an initiative that advocates for those in vulnerable communities abroad, bringing attention to the people in these parts of the world that do not have the access to the educational and medical resources we have the privilege of getting. I look forward to using these experiences to continue expanding my interest in global health. 

Vice President

Mariani Deluna

   My name is Mariani (Mari) Deluna and I am the Vice President of VAW for the 2016-2017 school year. I'm an avid, all-around volunteer, but I am mostly interested in volunteering in the medical field. I joined VAW to hone my skills in the field and gain more connections and experiences. I'm excited to visit a new country to learn more about new lifestyles and also to spread the awareness of the importance of medicine. I am also a musician and foodie!

Secretary

Brittany Davidson

   Hi I'm Brittany Davidson, secretary of VAW at UCSC. I'm an MCD Biology proposed major. I joined VAW because I am passionate about helping better the lives of others, especially those who need it most. I just want to make the world a better place and I think that starts with helping those most in need. I'm also passionate about science and the medical field in particular and I would love to get hands on experience. A combination of the two is like a dream come true for me. Some of my other hobbies/interests include running (I'm on the UCSC track team), hiking, and sleeping!

Publicity Chair
Elizabeth Hu
   Hi! I'm Lizzie Hu and I am currently a freshmen studying neuroscience at UCSC. Outside of VAW, I enjoy playing many sports including soccer, running, and swimming. I also like to participate in half-marathons with my family. :) I've always strived to help the community, from hosting blood drives and canned food drives to organizing charity fashion shows.  I was drawn to VAW because of all the opportunities it offers. I began cultivating an interest in the STEM field in high school, taking all the science courses that they offered from AP Chem/Biology to anatomy. VAW will lead me to further explore my interests through its incredible internship opportunities, which will not only give me experience but will also open my eyes to the real world and teach me valuable life skills.
Katelyn Ewe
   Hi! My name is Katelyn Ewe and I am a first year proposed molecular cell developmental biology major at UCSC. My reason for studying biology is that I believe it is the essence of all life, and I am awe struck by it. When I am taking a break from studying, I love hiking, running, and interacting with people. The one aspect I am most excited about experiencing is being able to help and educate people who have less opportunities. I joined Volunteers Around the World with the intention of touching people in ways that will remain with them for a life time. With that said, considering the bigger picture, I wish to leave this world a better place than when I first came to be. 
Vianne Kelly
   Hello my name is Vianne Kelly and I am a proposed Spanish Studies Major at UCSC. There is nothing I love more than being able to help people. I am from a small town in Southern California where my upbringing included an abundance of volunteer work for my community. I really want to be able to combine humanities with medicine in my future profession, which explains my major and why I am a part of VAW. Along with my leadership obligations in VAW at UCSC, I also enjoy aquatic sports (currently on the UCSC women's club water polo team), going to the beach and spending time with family.

Co-Social Media Chairs

Tiffany Phan

I am usually just an avid volunteer around San Francisco, and I am thankful that I have the opportunity to reach out and go abroad to help those in need.
