Trips with VAW@UCSC

Vietnam 2018
9/1 - 9/15

The VAW UCSC team will be traveling to Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam from September 1, 2018 to September 15, 2018! While in-country, we will have the opportunity to work with locals, visit a variety of communities, work with doctors, collaborate on several projects that touch upon community and leadership building, and of course, learn about the history and culture of Vietnam!
