A Trip Schedule: Panamá

Medical work days consist of any combination of mobile medical clinics, rotations (shadowing), visits to a local healthcare center, and/or participation in an instructional medical class taught by local health care professionals. Please note that this is just an example itinerary– each trip’s itinerary varies widely.

Day Description
1 arrive; spend night in Panama City
2 partial free day; overnight bus to Bocas
3 arrive at Bocas; training and orientation; rest day
4 Medical Work day 1 or free day
5 Medical Work day 2 or Education day
6 Medical Work Day 3 or Medical Work Day 1
7 Medical Work Day 4 or Medical Work Day 2
8 Medical Work Day 5 or free day
9 free day or Medical Work Day 3
10 Medical Work Day 6 or Medical Work Day 4
11 Education day or Medical Work Day 5
12 free day or Medical Work Day 6
13 partial free day; overnight bus to Panama City
14 arrive at Panama City; free day
15 return to USA

So what's covered?

Housing

A portion of the fee covers the volunteer house you will be staying at, or the cost on your host family's resources.

Food

A significant fraction of the fee covers food; your breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Clinic Transportation

You and your team will need transportation to and from the VAW clinic.

Airport Transportation (if flying with team)

You and your team will need transportation to and from the airport.

Staffing

VAW Global has staff operating the site that need to make a living too!
